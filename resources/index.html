Resources

  • Access4Bikes

    • This is a non-profit mountain bike advocacy group, recently renamed Marin Trail Stewards, dedicated to maintaining and expanding equitable singletrack trail access for mountain bikers in Marin County. They work with land managers, host trail workdays, and engage in political advocacy to champion sustainable trail development.

  • MCBC

    • This non-profit organization works to promote bicycling for transportation and recreation throughout Marin County. MCBC focuses on safer roads, connected bike routes, and education, supporting both road cycling and mountain biking interests in the region.

  • RHSMTB

    • This is a competitive high school mountain biking team from Redwood High School in Larkspur, California, participating in the NorCal Interscholastic Cycling League (NICA). It is a "no-cut" sport that focuses on building strong character, body, and mind through mountain bike riding and racing.

  • Strava

    • This is a popular social network and GPS-based application for athletes that allows cyclists and runners to track their activities, analyze performance data, and share their progress. A key feature is competing on "Segments," which are user-defined leaderboards for specific sections of a road or trail.
